Blog Post Zero
10/16/2023
I learned a whole lot about HTML and CSS in this assignment! This little project is the first real experience I have with HTML and CSS. The tools of web development are a lot less intimidating to me than they felt to me before starting this course. I like the markup language and I think so far it has been pretty intuitive to me. I wouldn’t say that I have encountered any significant challenges in making this webpage, just about any place I got stuck I was able to resolve pretty quickly by playing around with values and doing a bit of googling. Though to be fair, I am not doing anything especially complicated. I am most proud of being able to separate my main page into two halves, I think it looks much nicer than my selfie being below the text. By far the most useful resource in constructing this webpage would be the lectures I have so far attended on web development. I’ve also found a lot of very helpful information on the Ed Discussion forums.
